Three lightweight workshop moves
These replace the older standalone activity pages with simpler participant-facing suggestions aligned to the original workflow.


1 · Run
Have the ALDO conversation
Paste the prompt into a chatbot. Require it to ask clarifying questions before drafting. Watch for relationship, pre-assessment, strategic instruction, post-assessment, and reflection.


2 · Switch
Change the audience
Keep the same topic, then switch from teacher to coach or leader. Check whether the recommendations change in substance or only swap nouns.


3 · Audit
Verify the draft
Use the Strategy Reference and source files to check learning phase, SOLO level, effect-size claims, audience fit, and evidence of student movement.
