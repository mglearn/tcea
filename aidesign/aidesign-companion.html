Use sources before asking for output
What to attach or paste
  • The exact current TEKS student expectation or TEKS Guide excerpt.
  • A district scope-and-sequence note or required assessment target.
  • Visible Learning or local research notes for effect sizes.
  • Student work samples, if allowed by your district policy and stripped of identifying details.
What to tell the chatbot
Use only the attached sources. Ask for missing standards instead of inventing TEKS numbers. Label each instructional move with ALDO step, SOLO level, and learning phase.
